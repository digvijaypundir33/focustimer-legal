# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 12, 2026**

Digvijay Pundir ("we", "us", or "our") built the Focus Timer app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Focus Timer does not use any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or third-party data collection tools.

## Data Storage

All data created in Focus Timer (sessions, tasks, settings) is stored locally on your device. If you are signed into iCloud, your data may sync across your own devices via Apple's iCloud service. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to us.

## iCloud Sync

Focus Timer uses Apple's CloudKit framework to sync your data across devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Subscriptions

Focus Timer offers auto-renewable subscriptions (Focus Timer Pro). All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

## Children's Privacy

Focus Timer does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
